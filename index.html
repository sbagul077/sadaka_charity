About us

Sadaka Project is a 501(c)(3) tax exempt nonprofit organization with a mission to heal critically ill children and build healthcare capacity in the world.

We help mothers and children survive and thrive so they can look forward to a healthy old age. We ensure the safety of the air people breathe, the food they eat, the water they drink – and the medicines and vaccines they need.

Our core function is to direct and coordinate international health work through collaboration.

We support countries as they coordinate the efforts of governments and partners – including bi- and multilaterals, funds and foundations, civil society organizations and the private sector.

Promoting good health through the life-course cuts across all work done by Sadaka, and takes into account the need to address environment risks and social determinants of health, as well as gender, equity and human rights.
